Personal Favorite: Chicken Bowl

Cottage Cheese Salad

Ingredients: 1 cup of cottage cheese, diced tomatoes, cucumbers, red onion, avocado, chopped Greek olives, dill, oregano, crumbled feta, everything bagel seasoning, a drizzle of olive oil.

Calories: 350 | Protein: 20g | Carbs: 15g | Fat: 22g

Healthy Turkey Club Sandwich

Ingredients: 2 slices of uncured turkey bacon, 2 slices of whole grain bread (70 cal each), toast it for a minute, half a serving of light mayo, 1.5 oz of turkey, 1 slice of low calorie cheese, half a serving of light honey mustard, another 1.5 oz of turkey.

Calories: 450 | Protein: 35g | Carbs: 35g | Fat: 18g

Low Calorie Chicken Noodle Soup

Burger Bowl
